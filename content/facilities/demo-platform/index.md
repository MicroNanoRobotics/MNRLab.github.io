**Location:** Micro Nano Robotics Lab

**Responsible person:** Lab manager

* Two-Photon Polymerization (2PP) microfabrication
* Submicron-resolution 3D printing
* Crystal lattices, porous scaffolds, smooth contours, sharp edges, undercuts and bridges

Nanoscribe Photonic Professional GT2 uses Two-Photon Polymerization (2PP) to produce filigree structures of nearly any 3D shape by high-precision 3D printing. It can fabricate crystal lattices, porous scaffolds, naturally inspired patterns, smooth contours, sharp edges, undercuts and bridges with submicron resolution.

This platform supports ultra-precise and rapid scientific microfabrication for microrobotics, microfluidics, biofabrication, and related research.
