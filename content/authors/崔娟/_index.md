Juan Cui works on micro/nano sensing devices and systems, micro/nano manipulation, and related fields. She has published SCI papers in journals including Biofabrication, Acta Biomaterialia, ACS Applied Materials & Interfaces, Molecules, and Micromachines, and has twice received Best Paper Awards at EI-indexed international conferences. She was awarded the Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award of Beijing Institute of Technology and was named an Outstanding Graduate of Beijing.
