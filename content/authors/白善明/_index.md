Shanming Bai is a PhD student of Mechanical Engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology. His research focuses on magnetic continuum robots, magnetically controlled robots, and heterogeneous, multimaterial, high-precision photopolymerization-based manufacturing methods for magnetic devices.

## Representative Publications

1. Integration of triboelectric sensing and electromagnetic energy harvesting for self-adaptive vibration suppression, Nano Energy, 2023. (SCI Zone 1, IF: 19.069)

2. Electromagnetic-Triboelectric Energy Harvester Based on Vibration-to-Rotation Conversion for Human Motion Energy Exploitation, Applied Energy, 2022. (SCI Zone 1, IF: 11.446)
